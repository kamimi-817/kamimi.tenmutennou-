Emperor Tenmu

Hello! Here, we introduce Emperor Tenmu, the deity enshrined here.

Who is Emperor Tenmu?

Emperor Tenmu was the 40th Emperor of Japan and a historical figure who played an active role more than 1,300 years ago. He was a remarkable individual who organized Japan’s systems and greatly developed its culture. Because of these achievements, he is still revered as a deity today.

What to Ask Emperor Tenmu For

Emperor Tenmu is a deity who can help with various wishes.

  • Stability in Life: When you wish for stability in society or within an organization, it’s good to pray to Emperor Tenmu.
  • Growth in Studies and Culture: When you want to focus on your studies or deeply explore culture and the arts, praying to Emperor Tenmu might bring you new knowledge and ideas.
  • Overcoming Difficulties: When facing challenging problems, praying to Emperor Tenmu might give you the strength to solve them.

    • Emperor Tenmu’s Great Achievements

    What is "Ritsuryō"? Emperor Tenmu created a set of rules called "Ritsuryō." These were laws designed to unify the nation, much like modern-day laws. Thanks to these rules, people could live according to the same guidelines, bringing stability to society.

    A Cultural Hero: Emperor Tenmu also promoted the spread of Buddhism and the arts. During his reign, many temples and cultural institutions were established, enriching Japanese culture. It is thanks to Emperor Tenmu that the foundation of today’s Japanese culture was built.

    Offerings to Emperor Tenmu

    Suitable offerings to Emperor Tenmu include:

  • Sake (Japanese Rice Wine): Sake has been a special beverage since Emperor Tenmu’s time and is ideal for offerings.
  • Rice and Rice Products: As the staple food of Japan, rice is a basic offering.
  • Books and Stationery: In honor of Emperor Tenmu’s respect for culture, items related to learning and culture are appropriate.
